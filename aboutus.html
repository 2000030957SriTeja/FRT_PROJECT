A health system, also sometimes referred to as health care system, is the organization of people ,institutions ,and resources that deliver health care services to meet health needs of targeted population. Healthcare is the maintenance or improvement of health via prevention ,diagnosis, treatment , Recovery, or cure of disease ,illness ,injury and other physical or mental impairments in people. Medicine, dentistry ,pharmacy ,midwifery ,nursing, optometer ,audiology ,psychology ,occupational therapy ,physical therapy, athletic training, and other health professions are all part of health care. It includes work done in providing primary and secondary care and tertiary care, as well as in public health.
