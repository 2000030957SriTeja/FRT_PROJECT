Food to Avoid

  • Animal products: Liver and organ meats, egg yolks, whole milk, butter, cream,and whole milk cheeses
  • Vegetables high in saturated fats: Coconut, cocoa and palm
  • Other: Fried foods

Exercise

    High-intensity , interval-based
  • walking
  • swimming
  • light jogging

Precautions

  • Do not pull, push, or lift heavy objects
  • Do not do any sit-ups or push-ups
  • Do not attempt activities that create a pull or pain in the chest
